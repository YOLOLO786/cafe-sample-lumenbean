About LumenBean

LumenBean is a fictional cafe created as a portfolio sample. The design focuses on warm tones, soft typography and clear information hierarchy that makes it easy for small cafe owners to present their menu, gallery and reservation info online. Replace copy, menu items and images to fit a real business.
